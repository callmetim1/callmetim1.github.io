SO REALISTIC IT'S CREEPY.

DIVINE TERROR offers a range of attractions from super-scary rollercoasters to super-realistic 4D walks. Taste your fears, and see how much you can handle.

In this park, we have 7 rides:

1. The 9 Levels of Hell, a humongous high-speed jet-coaster going through different scenes of distress and horrors.

2. Curse of Saruhman, a labyrinth maze puzzle based on Curses and mazes (includes stealing a cart and travelling along rails.

3. The Path of Destruction, another labyrinth based on a variation of phobias, affecting many people.

4. Psychedelic Circus, a walk through a hypnotic circus house amongst a gang of evil clowns. 5. Fall of Dismay, another super-speed jet-coaster with many loop-the-loops and quick turns, and jump-scares.

6. Hall of Fear, a 4D walk through several different settings, with stories and experiences based on Creepypasta stories.

7. FRIGHT!, a rollercoaster tower of heights.

In fact, we also have a food court, or in other words, a food Tower, conveniently named "Heaven". Along the bottom, you might be able to catch a glimpse of Sam trying to climb the Tower.
